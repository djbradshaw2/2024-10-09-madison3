USDA-ARS IT

Installation of programs on government computers require administrative access to intall.

  1. Please download R and Rstudio software to the device you will use for the workshop using the link above.
  2. Open a Remedy Ticket and follow the steps below:
    1. Connect to VPN.
    2. Go to Axon+ - Type https://usdagcc.sharepoint.com/sites/ARS-Axon into your browser.
    3. Click on Application Launchpad > CEC Digital Workplace.
    4. Click on ARS Field Support Bundle
    5. Click on Request Now.
    6. Choose Outage/Issue (NOT Request Installation).
    7. Choose Hardware or Software and Applications.
    8. Enter your computer name (Search Name on your taskbar, or Apple search feature).
    9. Supervisor should auto populate, if not, type it in.
    10. Click Submit Request.
  3. IT will contact you to schedule a time for them to remotely install the software on your device.
